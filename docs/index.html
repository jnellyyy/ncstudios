Terms — bookings, payments & delivery

Effective from May 2026

Terms & conditions.

These terms apply to NC Studio wedding photography, videography, content and related creative packages.

Where an invoice, email, WhatsApp message or other written communication confirms a payment date, that date forms the agreed payment date. If the date is missed, the payment is overdue and late fees may apply.

01Booking confirmation

A date is not secured until NC Studio confirms the booking in writing and the required booking fee, retainer or deposit has cleared.

02Payments and late fees

Balances must be paid by the date shown on the invoice or agreed in writing by email, WhatsApp or message. Missed dates may pause delivery, release unpaid add-ons and lead to late fees, admin charges or statutory recovery costs where permitted by law.

03Retainers and deposits

Booking fees and retainers cover planning time and holding the date. They are normally non-refundable unless NC Studio is unable to provide the agreed service.

04Postponements

Postponed dates are subject to availability. If the new date is unavailable, the booking may be treated as a cancellation. Extra costs may apply where plans, travel or coverage change.

05Cancellations

Cancellation terms depend on the package, date, work already completed and notice given. Any money owed for completed planning, shooting, editing, travel or outsourced costs remains payable.

06Coverage limits

NC Studio will work carefully and professionally, but specific shots cannot be guaranteed where timings, access, weather, venue rules, guests, lighting or schedule changes affect what can be captured.

07Client responsibilities

Clients must provide accurate timings, locations, permissions, supplier details and key shot priorities in good time. Delays or missing information may affect the final coverage.

08Editing and creative style

NC Studio keeps creative control over shooting, selection, colour, pacing, music choice and final edit style. Raw files are not included unless agreed in writing before booking.

09Delivery

Delivery timings are estimates unless a fixed deadline is agreed in writing. Final galleries, films, previews and add-ons may be held until all outstanding balances are paid.

10Image use and privacy

NC Studio may use selected work for portfolio, website, social media and marketing unless privacy is agreed in writing. Client galleries and delivery links should not be shared publicly unless intended for that purpose.

11Storage and backups

Files are backed up with reasonable care, but clients should download and safely store delivered files once received. Long-term hosting or re-delivery may not always be available.

12Liability

If something outside NC Studio’s reasonable control prevents full service delivery, liability is limited to the amount paid for the affected service, except where the law says otherwise.
