Call structure

Keep the call focused: understand the day, confirm full day if it fits, then move to deposit.

1. Warm start

Thank her for making time and say you are excited to hear more about the day.

2. Confirm the basics
  • Wedding date
  • Ceremony and reception locations
  • Guest count and key times
  • Whether prep is nearby
3. Understand the day

Ask her to talk through the day from morning to evening.

4. What matters most

"When you imagine watching your wedding film back, what moments do you really want to see?"

5. Explain the best package

Full day coverage makes sense when ceremony, reception and both outfit changes need space.

6. Deliverables
  • Up to 10 hours coverage
  • Cinematic highlight film
  • Full ceremony film
  • Full speeches film
  • Online delivery
7. Price

"My full day videography package is £950. It gives you enough coverage so the day does not feel rushed and the key moments are properly captured."

8. Budget pause

"Does that feel within what you had in mind for videography?"

9. Booking

Deposit, balance due date, contract, booking form, and date only secured once deposit and agreement are done.

10. Close

"I would love to capture it for you. The next step would be for me to send over the booking details, contract and deposit info so we can secure the date."
