NC 35mm FILM PRINT LUT
=======================

File
----
NC_35mm_Film_Print_Rec709.cube

Designed for
------------
- Standard Rec.709 footage or footage already converted to Rec.709
- DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, CapCut, and other
  editors that support .cube 3D LUTs

Look
----
- Soft highlight roll-off
- Firm, gently colored shadows without crushed blacks
- Subtle cyan shadow density and warm highlights
- Rounded reds/skin tones
- Restrained digital greens and blues

Recommended strength
--------------------
Start at 60-75%. Use 100% when the source is neutral and evenly exposed.

Preview
-------
NC_35mm_Film_Print_preview.png shows untreated color on the left and the LUT
result on the right.

Important
---------
This is a creative look LUT, not a camera log conversion LUT. Convert Log,
S-Log, C-Log, V-Log, BRAW, or other camera color spaces to Rec.709 first,
then place this LUT after that conversion.

A LUT cannot add grain, halation, gate weave, or film texture. For a fuller
film treatment, add subtle 35mm grain and restrained halation after the LUT.
